Garlic Bread Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 large loaf of Italian bread or French bread
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a small bowl, combine softened butter, crushed garlic, olive oil, Parmesan cheese, chopped parsley, salt, and red pepper flakes if using. Mix until well blended.
  3. Cut the bread in half horizontally. Spread the butter mixture evenly over both halves of the bread.
  4. Place the bread on a baking sheet, and bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are slightly crispy and the butter is bubbly.
  5. Remove from the oven and slice into individual pieces. Serve warm.
