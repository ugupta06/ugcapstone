Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs
  • 100g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 200g pancetta or guanciale, cubed
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled
  • Black pepper, freshly ground
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add spaghetti and cook until al dente, about 8-10 minutes. Reserve a cup of pasta water and then drain the spaghetti.
  2. In a mixing bowl, whisk together eggs and Parmesan until well combined.
  3. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the pancetta with a little olive oil until it starts to get crispy, about 3-4 minutes. Add the garlic and cook for another minute. Remove garlic and discard.
  4. Reduce heat to low and add the drained spaghetti to the skillet. Toss well to coat the spaghetti in the pancetta fat.
  5. Remove the skillet from heat. Quickly pour the egg and cheese mixture into the pasta, whisking vigorously to prevent the eggs from scrambling. If the pasta seems too dry, add a little reserved pasta water.
  6. Season generously with freshly ground black pepper and serve immediately. Garnish with more grated Parmesan if desired.
