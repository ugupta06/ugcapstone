Bruschetta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 6 or 7 ripe tomatoes (about 1 1/2 lbs)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon balsamic vinegar
  • 6-8 fresh basil leaves, chopped.
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 baguette French bread or similar Italian bread
  • 1/4 cup olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Using a sharp knife, dice the tomatoes and put them in a mixing bowl. Add the minced garlic, 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil, balsamic vinegar, and chopped basil. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Stir the mixture well and set aside or let it marinate while you prepare the bread.
  4. Cut the baguette on the diagonal into 1/2 inch thick slices. Brush one side of each slice with olive oil and place on a baking sheet.
  5. Toast the bread in the preheated oven for 5-6 minutes, until the bread just begins to turn golden brown.
  6. Remove from oven and spread each slice with the tomato mixture. Serve immediately.
